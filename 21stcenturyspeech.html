The 21st Century Cup Public Speaking Global Invitational is recognized as one of China's most influential youth public speaking competitions. Operating under the auspices of UNESCO, the program supports international educational cooperation, cultural exchange, and youth development in alignment with UNESCO's core values. It is an international youth public speaking competition jointly organized by 21st Century and China Daily, the largest English-language news organization in China, under the Youth Pulse Global Exchange Initiative.

The program brings together outstanding young speakers from around the world to engage in dialogue on global issues, strengthen leadership and communication skills, and foster meaningful cross-cultural understanding. Students selected through the Regional Tryouts (May 2026) will have the opportunity to advance to the Global Invitational Finals, to be held in Hong Kong in August 2026. The competition is open to students aged 7–18, with divisions for Elementary, Middle School, and High School participants.
